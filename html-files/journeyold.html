Her Journey

1971 -1974

THE BIRTH

Born on May 20, in the quaint village of Vasai to parents Nazareth and Bela who fought against all odds to marry and create a beautiful world for themselves and their children.

1975-1981

HAPPY CHILDHOOD

Shifted to Goregaon, to a rental four-room home, which had a fruit garden, big verandah & 24 hours water connection. Saviorakesh and Paulritesh, his two younger brothers were born. A cheerful childhood indeed.

1982 -1987

THE DARK NIGHT

Moved from the 900 sq. ft. house in Goregaon to a one-room house of 180 sq.ft in the infamous area of Malvani, Malad. Dismayed, that night the child in him grew up. After school hours, he supported his family by working with Disco Bindiya for Rs. 3.5 per week.

1988 -1990

SUPPORTING THE FAMILY

Worked as an Office Assistant in a Pharmaceutical Company in Vile Parle (East), Mumbai. Tutored students during his 11th and 12th grade. Sold the highest number of LIC policies during his 1st year of graduation.

1991-1992

THE DOOR-TO-DOOR SALESMAN

Entered the biggest learning playfield of his life. Became a door-to-door salesperson for cosmetics, perfumes, garments, shoes, sandals and mats during his 2nd & 3rd year of graduation.
